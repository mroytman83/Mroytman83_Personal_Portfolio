About Me

Since I was very young, I have been obsessed with how movies encompass societal personas in their characters. I spent hours building LEGO arenas and creating extensive lore for minifigures that would embody different people's personalities. As an immigrant to the United States, I became increasingly aware of sociological constructs that governed daily life, especially how they translated to the digital landscape. I became very interested in studying and modeling human behavior using computational statistics. From there, I fell in love with building modern data-centric & AI applications for accessibility and growth!



I recently graduated from the University of Wisconsin, Madison with a degree in Data Science and Economics, where my coursework included foundation and applications of machine learning, big data, object-oriented programming, mathematics, statistics and econometrics.



I am interested in opportunities in health/fitness tech and financial markets, and I am prepared for a dynamic career in these fields. Feel free to contact me for any opportunities or collaborations through the contact tab!
